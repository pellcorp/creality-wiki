Why SimpleAF?

Modern probe support

Cartographer (V3/V4), Beacon, BTT Eddy, EddyNG, BLTouch, Microprobe, and Klicky — all installed by the same script. Pick your hardware, follow one guide.

Standard Klipper

A Klipper fork that keeps standard Klipper behaviour intact — no hidden blobs, no rewritten macros.

Open and editable

Source on GitHub. All macros and configuration live in plain files in your config directory — read them, edit them, override them.

Active community

Discord with per-probe channels — real users helping real users.
